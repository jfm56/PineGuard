Fire & Evacuation Preparedness Guide

Essential Preparedness Steps

  • Create a detailed evacuation plan for your family
  • Maintain a 30-foot defensible space around your home
  • Keep emergency supplies ready (water, food, first-aid kit)
  • Sign up for local emergency alerts

Pine Barrens Fire Safety

The Pine Barrens ecosystem is naturally fire-dependent, but this makes it particularly vulnerable during dry seasons.
